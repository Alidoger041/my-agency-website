Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and processes

What services does TechNex Solutions provide?

We offer a comprehensive range of IT services including Web Development, Mobile App Development, UI/UX Design, Digital Marketing, SaaS Solutions, and Cloud Infrastructure management.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies based on project complexity. A standard corporate website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while complex e-commerce or web applications may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages. This includes security updates, content updates, performance monitoring, and technical support to ensure your digital product keeps running smoothly.

What is your pricing model?

We offer both project-based fixed pricing and hourly rates depending on the nature of the work. For SaaS products and long-term support, we also have retainer and subscription models available.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients globally. We utilize modern collaboration tools to ensure seamless communication and project management regardless of time zones.
